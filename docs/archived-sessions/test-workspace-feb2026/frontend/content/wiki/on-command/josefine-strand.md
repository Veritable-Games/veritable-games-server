# Josefine "Joe" Strand

## Overview

**Josefine "Joe" Strand** is a Scout/Reconnaissance Expert and Infiltration
Specialist in the ON COMMAND extended squad pool. Born with unmatched enthusiasm
for life and determination to explore the old world, she brings optimism and
tactical expertise to dangerous reconnaissance missions.

## Background

Josefine joined the liberation team on what appeared to be a whim, but her
participation reveals deeper purpose. Her adventurous nature and reconnaissance
skills make her invaluable for gathering intelligence and accessing dangerous
territories. Despite her harsh origins, she maintains remarkable optimism that
inspires team morale.

## Character Profile

### Personality Complex

- **Surface**: Adventurous spirit with infectious enthusiasm for exploration
- **Depth**: Carries survivor's guilt from her background that drives her
  forward
- **Motivation**: Born with determination to explore and document the old world
- **Impact**: Maintains team morale through genuine optimism despite harsh
  realities

### Combat Specialization

- **Primary Role**: Scout and Reconnaissance Expert
- **Secondary Role**: Infiltration Specialist
- **Mission Focus**: Advanced reconnaissance, stealth infiltration, enemy
  position identification
- **Tactical Value**: Essential for gathering intelligence and accessing
  restricted areas

## Character Arc

### Origins and Background

While specific details of Joe's harsh origins remain partially mysterious, her
ability to maintain optimism despite adversity suggests significant personal
resilience. Her survivor's guilt motivates her forward rather than holding her
back, transforming potential trauma into tactical advantage.

### Exploration Drive

Her determination to explore the old world connects to the larger mission themes
of recovering lost technology and understanding historical context. This makes
her a natural ally to Vivian's research goals while providing unique
perspectives on discovered locations.

### Team Integration

Joe's enthusiasm serves as emotional counterbalance to the squad's heavier
psychological burdens. Her ability to find hope and wonder in dangerous
situations helps maintain team cohesion during difficult missions.

## Combat Effectiveness

### Reconnaissance Strengths

- **Stealth Operations**: Expert at accessing restricted areas without detection
- **Intelligence Gathering**: Skilled at assessing enemy positions and tactical
  situations
- **Environmental Navigation**: Exceptional ability to find routes through
  dangerous terrain
- **Communication**: Effective at relaying critical information under pressure

### Infiltration Capabilities

- **Access Skills**: Can penetrate enemy installations and restricted facilities
- **Observation Training**: Notices important details that others might miss
- **Adaptability**: Adjusts tactics based on changing environmental conditions
- **Survival Skills**: Maintains effectiveness in harsh or dangerous conditions

### Team Support

- **Early Warning**: Provides advance notice of enemy movements and positions
