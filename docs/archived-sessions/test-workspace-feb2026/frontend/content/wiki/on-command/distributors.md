<p><strong>Distributors</strong> are automated resource distribution systems in ON COMMAND that manage the allocation and delivery of equipment, supplies, and [[nanite-technology|nanite resources]] throughout operational areas.</p>
<h2>System Overview</h2>
<p>Distributors represent <strong>advanced logistical technology</strong> that automates resource management, enabling squads to focus on tactical objectives rather than supply chain management.</p>
