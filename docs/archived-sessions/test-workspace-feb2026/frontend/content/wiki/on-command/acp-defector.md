# ACP Defector (Name TBD)

## Overview

The **ACP Defector** is a Communications and Electronic Warfare Specialist
serving as a core squad member in the ON COMMAND liberation forces. As a former
ACP soldier who defected to the resistance, he provides crucial technical
expertise while navigating complex trust dynamics with his new teammates.

## Character Profile

### Personality Complex

- **Surface Tension**: Professional demeanor masking awareness of his precarious
  position within the squad
- **Internal Struggle**: Genuine commitment to liberation cause conflicting with
  squad members' natural distrust
- **Professional Pride**: Maintains technical competence and reliability to
  prove his value and loyalty
- **Identity Crisis**: Transitioning from enemy soldier to resistance fighter
  while maintaining personal integrity

### Combat Specialization

- **Primary Role**: Communications and Electronic Warfare Specialist
- **Background**: Former ACP military technology expert
- **Technical Expertise**: Electronic systems, communications, and
  counter-intelligence operations
- **Strategic Value**: Inside knowledge of ACP technology and tactical
  procedures

## Background & Defection

### ACP Military Service

His extensive service within ACP forces provides invaluable insider knowledge of
enemy technology, tactics, and operational procedures. This background makes him
simultaneously the squad's most valuable intelligence asset and their most
scrutinized member.

### Defection Motivation

The specific reasons for his defection remain partially mysterious, creating
ongoing tension about his true loyalties and motivations. His actions
consistently support the liberation cause, but squad members naturally question
whether this represents genuine conversion or sophisticated infiltration.

### Technical Expertise

His deep understanding of ACP technology systems makes him essential for
counter-intelligence operations and electronic warfare, while his communication
skills enable coordination with other resistance cells and intelligence
gathering from enemy sources.

## Character Arc

### Trust Building

The defector's primary character arc involves gradually earning the trust and
acceptance of his squadmates through consistent performance and demonstrated
loyalty. Each mission provides opportunities to prove his commitment to the
liberation cause.

### Identity Integration

Throughout the campaign, he must reconcile his ACP background with his new
identity as a resistance fighter, learning to balance valuable enemy knowledge
with genuine commitment to liberation goals.

### Professional Recognition

His growing integration into the squad parallels increasing recognition of his
technical competence and strategic value, as teammates learn to rely on his
expertise while accepting his complex background.

## Squad Integration

### Technical Leadership

- **Communications Systems**: Maintains squad communication security and
  coordinates with other resistance units
- **Electronic Warfare**: Conducts counter-intelligence operations and
  electronic attack on enemy systems
- **Intelligence Analysis**: Interprets ACP communications and provides
  strategic intelligence
- **Technology Integration**: Helps adapt captured ACP equipment for resistance
  use

### Trust Dynamics

- **Prove Loyalty**: Must continuously demonstrate commitment to liberation
  cause through actions and decisions
- **Valuable Asset**: Squad gradually recognizes his technical expertise and
  insider knowledge as crucial for mission success
- **Complex Relationships**: Navigate relationships with squadmates who have
  personal reasons to distrust former ACP personnel
- **Professional Competence**: Earns respect through consistent technical
  performance and tactical contributions

### Strategic Value

- **Enemy Intelligence**: Provides crucial insights into ACP tactics,
  technology, and operational procedures
- **Technical Countermeasures**: Develops defensive measures against ACP
  electronic warfare and communication interception
- **Tactical Advantage**: His knowledge enables squad to exploit ACP
  technological weaknesses and procedural vulnerabilities
- **Communication Security**: Ensures squad communications remain secure while
  maintaining contact with resistance networks

## Technical Capabilities

### Communications Expertise

- **Secure Communications**: Maintains encrypted communication networks for
  squad and resistance coordination
- **Signal Intelligence**: Intercepts and analyzes enemy communications for
  tactical and strategic intelligence
- **Network Security**: Protects resistance communication networks from ACP
  counter-intelligence operations
- **Coordination Systems**: Enables effective coordination between squad and
  other resistance units

### Electronic Warfare

- **System Infiltration**: Penetrates ACP electronic systems for intelligence
  gathering and sabotage operations
- **Counter-Intelligence**: Protects resistance operations from ACP electronic
  surveillance and tracking
- **Technology Adaptation**: Modifies captured ACP equipment for resistance use
  and tactical advantage
- **Electronic Attack**: Conducts offensive electronic warfare operations
  against enemy systems and communications

### Intelligence Operations

- **Enemy Analysis**: Interprets ACP operational patterns and tactical
  procedures based on insider knowledge
- **Technology Assessment**: Evaluates captured ACP equipment and develops
  tactical applications
- **Strategic Planning**: Provides intelligence input for mission planning and
  strategic decision-making
- **Risk Assessment**: Identifies potential security risks and ACP
  counter-intelligence threats
