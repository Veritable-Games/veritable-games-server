# Carden Frode

## Overview

**Carden Frode** is a Combat Medic and Field Surgeon serving as a core squad
member in the ON COMMAND liberation forces. As the team's primary medical
specialist, he carries the enormous responsibility of keeping his squadmates
alive during dangerous tactical operations.

## Character Profile

### Personality Complex

- **Surface Demeanor**: Deeply loyal and methodical under pressure
- **Internal Struggle**: Haunted by those he couldn't save in previous
  operations
- **Professional Approach**: Clinical precision balanced with underlying warmth
- **Emotional Burden**: Struggles with the weight of keeping others alive during
  combat

### Combat Specialization

- **Primary Role**: Combat Medic and Field Surgeon
- **Designation**: Blue team member
- **Medical Expertise**: Advanced battlefield trauma treatment and emergency
  surgery
- **Tactical Integration**: Provides medical support while maintaining combat
  effectiveness

## Background & Experience

### Military Medical Training

Carden's extensive medical training encompasses both civilian surgical
techniques and military battlefield medicine. His ability to perform life-saving
procedures under fire makes him invaluable to squad operations, but also places
enormous psychological pressure on his shoulders.

### Moral Conflicts

The intersection of medical ethics and military necessity creates ongoing
internal tension for Carden. His Hippocratic oath to "do no harm" conflicts with
the violent realities of liberation warfare, forcing him to constantly balance
healing with tactical requirements.

### Combat History

Previous operations have left Carden with deep emotional scars from squadmates
he was unable to save. These experiences drive both his dedication to improving
his skills and his fear of future failures that could cost lives.

## Character Arc

### Professional Development

Carden's journey involves mastering the balance between medical precision and
battlefield adaptation. His growing confidence in combat medicine parallels his
emotional growth in processing the traumatic aspects of wartime medical service.

### Relationship Dynamics

His role as the squad's lifeline creates unique relationships with each team
member. While they depend on his skills for survival, he depends on their
protection to perform his medical duties effectively.

### Psychological Evolution

Throughout the campaign, Carden learns to process his medical failures as
learning experiences rather than personal shortcomings, developing resilience
while maintaining his compassionate nature.

## Squad Integration

- **Life-or-Death Decisions**: Final authority on medical evacuation and
  treatment priorities
- **Tactical Input**: Medical considerations affect mission planning and risk
  assessment
- **Resource Management**: Manages medical supplies and evacuation procedures
- **Team Support**: Provides psychological support alongside physical healing

### Combat Effectiveness

- **Battlefield Medicine**: Performs emergency surgery under fire
- **Injury Assessment**: Rapid triage and treatment prioritization
- **Mobility Support**: Helps wounded squad members maintain combat
  effectiveness
- **Emergency Procedures**: Handles critical medical situations affecting
  mission success

### Team Relationships

- **Trust Building**: Squad members' lives literally depend on his medical
  competence
- **Protective Instincts**: Team members understand his value and provide extra
  protection
- **Professional Respect**: Earned through consistent life-saving performance
  under pressure
- **Emotional Support**: Serves as confidant for squad members dealing with
  combat stress

- **Emergency Medicine**: Advanced trauma treatment and stabilization techniques
