<h1>Suit Upgrades System</h1>
<p>The <strong>Suit Upgrades System</strong> enables contestants to replace natural body parts with mechanical components that provide statistical enhancements and tactical advantages, creating irreversible choices that fundamentally alter both competitive capabilities and social positioning within NOXII death games. This sophisticated augmentation framework forces contestants to sacrifice humanity for competitive advantage while creating permanent visual markers of participation in Imperial violence systems.</p>
<p>Unlike temporary equipment that provides tactical utility without permanent consequences, suit upgrades represent irreversible modifications that permanently alter contestant physiology and social identity. The upgrade system emphasizes long-term strategic decisions that affect both competitive performance and hub social dynamics through visible mechanical modifications that mark contestants as willing participants in their own dehumanization.</p>
<p>The upgrade framework operates on damage-dealt currency earned through violence against fellow prisoners, creating direct connections between harm inflicted upon others and personal enhancement opportunities. This economic structure forces contestants to confront the moral complexity of advancement through violence while providing genuine competitive advantages that incentivize continued participation in harm-based progression.</p>
