<h1>SEEKING (Want/Take)</h1>
<h2>Core Definition</h2>
<p><strong>SEEKING</strong> represents the fundamental human drive for desire, need, and acquisition. This Enact encompasses asking for objects, attention, knowledge, or any form of need fulfillment.</p>
<h2>Emotional Expression Patterns</h2>
<h3>Manifestations</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Object Desire</strong>: Wanting physical items, tools, or resources</li>
<li><strong>Attention Seeking</strong>: Need for social recognition, validation, or interaction</li>
<li><strong>Knowledge Seeking</strong>: Curiosity, learning drive, information gathering</li>
<li><strong>Experience Seeking</strong>: Desire for new experiences, adventures, or challenges</li>
<li><strong>Connection Seeking</strong>: Need for relationships, understanding, or belonging</li>
</ul>
<h3>Behavioral Indicators</h3>
<ul>
<li>Reaching out or leaning forward</li>
<li>Direct requests and asking behaviors</li>
<li>Exploratory actions and investigation</li>
<li>Active pursuit of goals or objectives</li>
<li>Open body language and engaged attention</li>
</ul>
<h2>Character Implementation Guidelines</h2>
<h3>In NOXII Context</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Resistance Information Gathering</strong>: Characters seeking intelligence about escape routes, guard patterns, or alliance opportunities</li>
<li><strong>Resource Acquisition</strong>: Need for better equipment, medical supplies, or strategic advantages</li>
<li><strong>Connection Building</strong>: Desire to build trust networks while maintaining performance cover</li>
</ul>
<h3>In AUTUMN Context</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Seasonal Needs</strong>: Characters seeking different things based on seasonal cycles</li>
<li><strong>Family Dynamics</strong>: Children seeking parental attention, parents seeking understanding</li>
<li><strong>Community Integration</strong>: Desire for belonging and acceptance within village relationships</li>
</ul>
<h3>In DODEC Context</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Knowledge Preservation</strong>: Community members seeking historical information or technical knowledge</li>
<li><strong>Resource Sharing</strong>: Collaborative seeking where individual desires serve community benefit</li>
<li><strong>Infrastructure Needs</strong>: Community seeking materials or assistance for collective projects</li>
</ul>
<h2>Dialogue Integration</h2>
<h3>Expression Patterns</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Direct Requests</strong>: "I need..." "Can you help me with..." "Where can I find..."</li>
<li><strong>Exploratory Questions</strong>: "What if..." "Have you ever..." "I wonder about..."</li>
<li><strong>Desire Statements</strong>: "I want to understand..." "I&#39;m looking for..." "I wish I could..."</li>
</ul>
<h3>Character Voice Variations</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Confident Seeking</strong>: Direct, assertive requests with clear goals</li>
<li><strong>Hesitant Seeking</strong>: Uncertain, cautious exploration of needs</li>
<li><strong>Desperate Seeking</strong>: Urgent, high-stakes need expression</li>
<li><strong>Curious Seeking</strong>: Wonder-driven, exploratory questioning</li>
</ul>
<h2>Cross-Project Applications</h2>
<h3>Universal Themes</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Growth and Learning</strong>: Characters seeking personal development</li>
<li><strong>Relationship Building</strong>: Desire for connection and understanding</li>
<li><strong>Problem Solving</strong>: Seeking solutions to challenges and obstacles</li>
<li><strong>Resource Management</strong>: Need for tools, information, or assistance</li>
</ul>
<h3>Cultural Variations</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Individual vs. Collective</strong>: Seeking personal benefit vs. community benefit</li>
<li><strong>Direct vs. Indirect</strong>: Cultural patterns of request-making and need expression</li>
<li><strong>Hierarchical vs. Egalitarian</strong>: How social structure affects seeking behavior</li>
</ul>
<h2>Implementation Notes</h2>
<h3>Animation and Visual Design</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Body Language</strong>: Reaching, leaning forward, open gestures</li>
<li><strong>Facial Expression</strong>: Alert, focused, engaged attention</li>
<li><strong>Symbol Design</strong>: Forward-moving arrows, open hands, question marks</li>
</ul>
<h3>Audio Design</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vocal Patterns</strong>: Rising intonation, questioning tone, active engagement</li>
<li><strong>Sound Effects</strong>: Footsteps moving toward objectives, searching sounds</li>
<li><strong>Musical Themes</strong>: Ascending melodies, exploratory harmonies</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><em>SEEKING serves as a fundamental driving force in character motivation and player engagement, representing the active pursuit of needs, desires, and goals across all project contexts.</em></p>
