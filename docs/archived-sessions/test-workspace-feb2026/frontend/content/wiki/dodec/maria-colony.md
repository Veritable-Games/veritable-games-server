# Maria Colony

## Overview

**Maria Colony** represents one of the most successful post-war communities on
[Planet Chione](planet-chione), serving as home to [The Maintenance
Sentinel](the Maintenance Sentinel) and a diverse population working together to
build a sustainable, cooperative society. Located in a region that showcases the
best of DODEC-era community organization, Maria demonstrates how human
settlements can thrive through mutual aid, technological integration, and
respect for both individual autonomy and collective welfare.
