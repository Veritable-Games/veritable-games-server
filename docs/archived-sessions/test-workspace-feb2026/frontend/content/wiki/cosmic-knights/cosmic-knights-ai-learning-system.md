# AI Learning System

**AI Learning System** in COSMIC KNIGHTS provides adaptive opposition and
training that evolves based on player tactics, creating increasingly
sophisticated challenges for Banner coordination.

## System Overview

The AI Learning System creates **dynamic, adaptive opposition** that responds to
player strategies, ensuring consistent challenge and encouraging tactical
innovation.

## Core Learning Mechanics

### Tactical Pattern Recognition

- **Strategy Analysis**: AI systems studying player Banner coordination patterns
  and tactical approaches
- **Adaptation Response**: Opposition tactics adjusting to counter player
  strategies and coordination
- **Innovation Recognition**: AI identifying and responding to player tactical
  innovations
- **Performance Tracking**: Continuous monitoring of player coordination
  effectiveness and development

### Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment

- **Challenge Scaling**: Opposition difficulty adapting to player Banner
  coordination skill level
- **Balanced Challenge**: Maintaining appropriate challenge without overwhelming
  player capabilities
- **Skill Recognition**: AI understanding player individual and team
  coordination proficiency
- **Progressive Enhancement**: Gradually increasing challenge as player skills
  develop
