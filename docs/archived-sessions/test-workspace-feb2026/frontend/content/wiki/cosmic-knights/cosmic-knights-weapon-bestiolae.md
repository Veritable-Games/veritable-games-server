# Bestiolae Magma Destroyer

**Bestiolae Magma Destroyer** is a high-intensity Banner weapon in COSMIC
KNIGHTS that utilizes molten energy and thermal manipulation for devastating
offensive capabilities and tactical area control.

## Weapon Overview

The Bestiolae Magma Destroyer represents **elemental fire and molten energy
mastery**, creating tactical opportunities through thermal manipulation while
serving as a powerful Banner coordination weapon.

- **Temperature Control**: Managing heat distribution for tactical advantage and
