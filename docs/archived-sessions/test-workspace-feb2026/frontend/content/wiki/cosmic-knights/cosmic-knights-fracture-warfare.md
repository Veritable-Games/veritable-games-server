# Fracture Warfare & Sealing

**Fracture Warfare & Sealing** represents the primary objective system in COSMIC
KNIGHTS, where Banner teams coordinate to close dimensional fractures while
engaging in tactical combat.

## System Overview

Fracture sealing creates **time-sensitive tactical objectives** that require
Banner coordination, combat effectiveness, and strategic resource management.

## Fracture Mechanics

### Dimensional Instability

**Core mechanics governing Fracture behavior:**

#### Fracture Characteristics

- **Dimensional Breach**: Openings between dimensions causing environmental
  instability
- **[[cosmic-knights-corruption-pressure|Corruption]] Generation**: Fractures
  creating progressive environmental degradation
- **Energy Emission**: Dimensional energy affecting local environment and
  tactical conditions
- **Instability Patterns**: Fracture behavior patterns affecting sealing
  strategy and timing

#### Sealing Requirements

- **[[cosmic-knights-banner-command|Banner Coordination]]**: Multiple Banner
  members working together for effective sealing
- **[[cosmic-knights-crystal-economy|Crystal]] Investment**: Resource
  expenditure required for sealing operations
- **Timing Coordination**: Precise timing requirements for successful Fracture
  closure
- **Environmental Management**: Managing corruption and instability during
  sealing operations

### Fracture Types

#### Minor Fractures

**Small dimensional breaches requiring basic sealing operations:**

- **Single Banner Sealing**: Manageable by individual Banner members with
  support
- **Limited Corruption**: Minimal environmental impact and time pressure
- **Basic Resource Requirements**: Standard crystal and equipment investment
- **Training Opportunities**: Ideal for developing sealing skills and
  coordination

#### Major Fractures

**Significant dimensional breaches requiring coordinated Banner operations:**

- **Team Coordination**: Requiring multiple Banner members working in
  coordination
- **Significant Corruption**: Major environmental impact and time pressure
- **Resource Intensive**: Substantial crystal and equipment investment required
- **Strategic Challenge**: Requiring advanced tactical planning and execution

#### Critical Fractures

**Large dimensional breaches requiring full Banner coordination:**

- **Full Team Required**: All available Banner members needed for successful
  sealing
- **Extreme Corruption**: Severe environmental impact and urgent time pressure
- **Maximum Resources**: Requiring significant crystal and equipment investment
- **Elite Challenge**: Testing advanced Banner coordination and tactical
  capabilities
