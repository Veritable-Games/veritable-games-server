# Enact Dialogue System

The **Enact Dialogue System** represents a revolutionary emotional conversation
framework that implements twelve fundamental emotional forces through binary
directional choices, creating authentic human emotional expression validated by
extensive psychological research. Unlike traditional dialogue trees with
predetermined responses, Enact enables genuine emotional communication through
mathematical frameworks that generate over 1,512 distinct emotional expressions
while maintaining interface simplicity through left/right (LT/RT) selection.

The system operates through **emotional force dynamics** where twelve core
emotions including SEEKING, EMPATHY, CONTENTMENT, ELATION, SUFFERING, ASSERTION,
OPPOSITION, FEAR, DISGUST, ANTICIPATION, ACCEPTANCE, and COMFORT interact
through intensity modulation, temporal bias, and authenticity strain. This
framework creates emergent emotional complexity from simple binary choices,
enabling nuanced expression that reflects real human psychological processes
rather than scripted responses.

Enact emphasizes **psychological authenticity** through mixed emotion processing
where primary and secondary emotions blend at validated ratios, creating
realistic emotional ambivalence and complexity. The system incorporates
processing delays, authenticity strain from expressive dissonance, and resource
exhaustion mechanics that create naturalistic conversation flow while preventing
unlimited emotional cycling that characterizes traditional dialogue systems.

## Twelve Emotional Forces Framework

### Core Emotional Forces

**SEEKING** represents approach drive toward desired objects, attention, or
knowledge, creating conversational momentum through curiosity and desire for
connection or information. Seeking drives exploration while providing natural
conversation initiation through interest expression and engagement
demonstration.

**EMPATHY** enables understanding and reciprocating emotional energy through
resonance with others' emotional states, creating connection through shared
feeling and mutual understanding. Empathy facilitates relationship building
while demonstrating emotional intelligence through appropriate emotional
response and support provision.

**CONTENTMENT** expresses satisfaction, peace, and equilibrium states that
communicate comfort with current circumstances and relationship dynamics.
Contentment provides emotional baseline while enabling appreciation expression
and stability communication through calm presence.

**ELATION** manifests joy, excitement, and celebration through high-energy
positive expression that shares happiness and creates emotional contagion.
Elation enables enthusiasm communication while building positive relationship
dynamics through shared celebration and excitement transmission.

**SUFFERING** consolidates sadness and pain into distress expression seeking
comfort, creating vulnerability through emotional need communication. Suffering
enables support seeking while building intimacy through shared vulnerability and
emotional authenticity.

**ASSERTION** combines self-worth, principles, and courage into confident
expression that establishes boundaries and communicates values. Assertion
enables autonomy expression while creating respect through clear communication
and principled stance taking.

### Opposition and Defensive Forces

**OPPOSITION** consolidates anger and defensive response into rejection and
boundary-setting that protects emotional space and communicates disagreement.
Opposition enables conflict navigation while maintaining relationship integrity
through clear boundary communication and defensive positioning.

**FEAR** expresses vulnerability and uncertainty while seeking reassurance,
creating connection through shared concern and support seeking. Fear enables
threat communication while building trust through vulnerability expression and
protection seeking.

**DISGUST** communicates moral or physical revulsion through contamination
rejection that establishes value boundaries and ethical positions. Disgust
enables moral communication while creating social boundaries through rejection
expression and value assertion.

**ANTICIPATION** focuses on future-oriented preparation and expectation,
creating forward momentum through planning and possibility exploration.
Anticipation enables future navigation while building shared vision through
expectation communication and preparation coordination.

**ACCEPTANCE** expresses yielding, surrendering, and flow states that
communicate flexibility and adaptation to circumstances. Acceptance enables
conflict resolution while demonstrating wisdom through situation acknowledgment
and resistance release.

**COMFORT** provides soothing and safety to others through nurturing expression
that creates emotional security and support. Comfort enables caregiving while
building trust through safety provision and emotional support demonstration.

## Binary Choice Interface

### LT/RT Selection System

**Top Force Mapping** assigns the two strongest emotional forces to left and
right triggers, creating intuitive selection through dominant emotion
presentation rather than menu navigation. Force mapping simplifies interface
while maintaining complexity through emergent combination and contextual
variation.

**Contextual Weight Calculation** determines force strength through personality,
energy level, cultural configuration, relationship context, and temporal bias
that creates situation-appropriate options. Weight calculation creates variety
while ensuring relevant choices through comprehensive context integration.

**Choice Difficulty Indication** communicates decision complexity through visual
feedback including selection time, option similarity, and emotional conflict
that helps players understand choice significance. Difficulty indication
supports decision-making while creating tension through complexity
acknowledgment.

**Selection Confirmation Feedback** provides immediate response through
character expression, voice modulation, and body language that communicates
chosen emotion before verbal response. Confirmation feedback creates
responsiveness while demonstrating emotion through multiple channels.

**Recursive Feedback Integration** enables all systems to influence each other

**Constraint-Based Authenticity** creates realistic behavior through resource

## Resource Exhaustion Framework

### Conversation Depletion Mechanics

**Emotional Range Reduction** decreases available emotional forces as
conversation continues, creating natural conversation conclusion through option
limitation rather than arbitrary ending. Range reduction creates closure while
reflecting real conversation fatigue through progressive constraint.

**Specificity Degradation** shifts from specific to vague expression as
resources deplete, creating realistic conversation decay through detail loss and
generalization increase. Specificity degradation creates naturalism while
preventing infinite conversation through quality reduction.

**Future Orientation Loss** reduces anticipation and planning expression as
exhaustion increases, creating present-focus through temporal range reduction.
Orientation loss reflects fatigue while creating conversation conclusion through
future discussion inability.

**Stress-Accelerated Consumption** increases resource depletion rate during
high-intensity emotional exchange, creating shorter conversations during
conflict or crisis. Accelerated consumption creates variety while reflecting
real emotional exhaustion through intensity impact.

### Recovery and Restoration

**Time-Based Regeneration** restores conversational resources through rest
periods between interactions, creating natural conversation spacing through
recovery requirements. Regeneration prevents spam while encouraging meaningful
interaction through resource management.

**Activity-Based Recovery** enables resource restoration through specific
activities including solitude, creative expression, and physical activity that
create gameplay integration. Activity recovery creates variety while supporting
character routines through recovery behavior.

**Relationship-Specific Pools** maintains separate resource pools for different
relationships, preventing single conversation from exhausting all social
capacity. Specific pools create sustainability while enabling multiple
relationships through resource separation.

**Emergency Reserve Access** provides limited additional resources during
critical conversations through reserve activation that enables important
discussions despite exhaustion. Reserve access creates drama while maintaining
stakes through limited availability.
